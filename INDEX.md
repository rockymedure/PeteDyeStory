# Pete Dye Golf Club Story - Compendium

A collection of posts from Jimmy LaRosa's Substack documenting the history and development of the Pete Dye Golf Club in West Virginia.

**Source:** [Jimmy LaRosa's Substack](https://jimmylarosa.substack.com)

**About:** While developing the Pete Dye Golf Club, they discovered 250 years of history buried in the WV hills—Indian moccasins, miners' boots, and golfers' spikes. This newsletter tells that story.

## Timeline Overview

The Pete Dye Golf Club story spans decades:
- **Early Years:** The vision and initial development
- **Construction:** 18 years to complete the course
- **1994-95:** A turning point for the club
- **Late 1990s:** Grand opening, national recognition, tournaments
- **2000:** WV Open hosted at the club
