# Revision Map: Seth's Rough Cut → The Film We're Making

**Target runtime:** 20–25 minutes  
**Current runtime:** 28:55

## The Problem

The current cut doesn't tell the story. It's a portrait of the golf course as it exists today — beautiful footage, people reflecting on how special the place is. If you showed it to someone who's never heard of Pete Dye and asked "what happened?", they couldn't tell you. They'd know the place is beautiful. They wouldn't know how it got there.

We need to take the audience through what happened, step by step, beginning to end. The footage from your cut is in this film — but it comes at the end, as the reward, not at the beginning.

**The story comes first.** If we have the footage, we use it. If we don't, we generate it with AI video, faithful to the interviews, banquet tapes, construction footage, and family accounts. Nothing invented.

### What needs to change

1. **Open on devastation, not beauty.**
2. **Add 45 seconds of "Who Is Pete Dye?"** before the rejection.
3. **Expand the middle.** 5 minutes of generic struggle becomes 5–7 minutes of specific crises.
4. **Move hospitality and gates to the closing.**
5. **Add Opening Day with dates.** July 4, 1993. 1994. July 4, 1995.
6. **Add the bell on #12.** The emotional climax. Not in the cut at all right now.
7. **Rework the closing:** "Our spot" → Pete's line → drone sunrise → bell → title card.
8. **Lower thirds.** The audience needs to know who Jimmy is by minute 2.
