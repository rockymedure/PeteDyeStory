# The Sunrise We Waited 17 Years For

**Date:** November 30, 2025  
**Author:** Jimmy LaRosa

---

*Celebrating the long-awaited opening of all 18 holes and the people who made it possible.*

This post celebrates the milestone of opening all 18 holes at the Pete Dye Golf Club after 17 years of development. It focuses on:

- The emotional significance of finally completing the full 18-hole course
- Recognition of the people who made the dream a reality
- The journey from initial vision to completed golf course
- Reflections on what the achievement meant to the team and members
